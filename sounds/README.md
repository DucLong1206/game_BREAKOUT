# Sounds for Breakout Game

Add the following sound files to this directory:

- background.mp3 or background.ogg: Background music (looping)
- hit_brick.wav or hit_brick.mp3: Sound when ball hits a brick
- hit_paddle.wav or hit_paddle.mp3: Sound when ball hits the paddle
- hit_boss.wav or hit_boss.mp3: Sound when ball hits the boss
- powerup.wav or powerup.mp3: Sound when collecting a power-up

You can find free sound effects online or create your own.

For background music, ensure it's royalty-free and suitable for looping.
